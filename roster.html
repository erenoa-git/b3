              0. Square machines and declined sales 
             During a card sale, please take a few extra seconds
             to confirm the payment goes through successfully. It's
             more troublesome to run after people and get them to
             come back. It'll also teach customers to wait until the
             card company approves the sale, before they head off.
             If square doesn't say "confirmed", but the customer's
             phone says "paid", this means Square didn't process it
             (and the phone confirmed too early). However we can't do
             anything about this besides telling customer "Please
             check later and if you weren't charged, please come back"
